Generative Commerce

James Tenniswood February 4, 2025
 

Goal
Empower customers to make confident buying decisions.

Customer

Challenges
Lack of knowledge
Overwhelmed with choice
Decision paralysis

Situation
Not confident enough to make a purchase without further research
Going offsite to build knowledge, unlikely to comeback later.
When the consumer is confident, engagement is often for price comparison only (race to the bottom)

Business

Challenges
Reduce abandonment
Increase conversion
Boost cross sell/up sell motion
Increase brand relevance

Situation
Build on strong brand recognition
Leverage existing customer relationships
Create value from customer starting their shopping journeys (top of funnel)

Features

Conversational guided browse
Intelligent contextual product comparison

Generative buyers guides
